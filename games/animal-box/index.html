Animal Box

Match adorable animals with the sounds they make and expand your budding reader's vocabulary.

This mini-game is part of the Abe's Spelling Fun collection. When the full web app is ready, this page will launch the interactive Phaser experience where kids drag animals into the correct sound box.

Until then, you can explore the project roadmap and assets in the repository while the engineering team brings everything together for the public release.

  • Drag-and-drop gameplay that reinforces letter-sound relationships.
  • Gentle encouragement and cheerful effects to celebrate correct matches.
  • Built with accessibility in mind for young learners.
